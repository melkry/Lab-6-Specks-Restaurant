Welcome

Welcome to Speck's Restaurant, located in the charming town of Amherstburg, Ontario! Our family-owned establishment has been a beloved staple in the community for over four decades, serving up delicious, homemade meals made with love.

But Speck's isn't just a place to grab a delicious meal - it's a gathering place for friends and family. Our cozy, inviting atmosphere is the perfect setting for intimate dinners, group celebrations, and everything in between.

We take great pride in our commitment to excellence, and we're passionate about creating memorable experiences for our guests. So come on in and experience the warmth and hospitality of Speck's Restaurant - we can't wait to welcome you!
